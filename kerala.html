MUNNAR



  • About:

    • It is referred to as the tea heaven of South India because of the extensive amount of tea plantations in this area which offer natural beauty to the place.Evidently,this place is famous for tea and spice plantation tour.

  • Best time to visit: October to March

  • Nearest airport: Kochi Airport is 143 km away.

  • Nearest railway station: Aluva Railway Station is 110 km away.
  • other attractions:Attukad Waterfalls.Mattupetty Dam,TATA Tea Museum

KOCHI



  • About:

    • Fort kochi is also referred to as queen of Arabian sea.Kochi is a place which is a must visit.you can explore museums,palaces,temples,art galleries,beaches,cuisines and much more.

  • Best time to visit: September to March

  • Nearest Airport: Kochi International Airport in the city.

  • Nearest Railway Station: Ernakulam Junction is 13 km away.

  • other attractions:Chinese fishing nets,Marine Drive,Bolgatty Palace

ALLEPPEY



  • About:

    • Alleppey is one of the best tourist attractions in Kerala.It is known for the backwaters.Going for a houseboat vacation in the scenic landscape gives a soothing effect.

  • Best time to visit: September to mid-May

  • Nearest airport: Kochi Airport at 53 km.

  • Nearest railway station: Alleppey Railway Station is in the city.

  • Other attractions:Alappuzha Beach,Revi Karunakaran Museum,Ambalappuzha Sree Krishna Temple

KOZHIKODE



  • About:

    • It is a paradise for those who wish to explore nature and history at its best. It has churches, temples, historic monuments,sanctuaries and waterfalls. thus,Kozhikode has everything to make it a perfect holiday in Kerala.

  • Best time to visit: September to May

  • Nearest airport: Karipur International Airport in Calicut is just 28 km away.

  • Nearest railway station: Kozhikode Railway Station is well within the city limits.

THIRUVANANTHAPURAM



  • About:

    • Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of kerala and is among the cleanest and most beautiful places of india.There are various historical monuments to visit in this city.there are beautiful beaches along with serene backwaters fringed by palm and coconut groves

  • Best time to visit: October to February.

  • Nearest airport: Trivandrum International Airport in the city.

  • Nearest railway station: Railway station in the city.

THEKKADY



  • About:

    • Thekkady is most famous for Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.Periyar is the perfect destination for wildlife enthusiasts and for anyone who wants to explore the misty mountains of the Nilgiris

  • Best time to visit: November to early-May

  • Nearest airport: Karipur International Airport at 95 km.

  • Nearest railway station: Kottayam Railway Station at 114 km.

  • other attractions:Periyar Lake,Mullaperiyar Dam,Deepa World Spice Ayurvedic

IDUKKI



  • About:

    • it is a very important tourist destination in kerala.this district of Kerala has cascading waterfalls, mesmerizing tea gardens, rolling green hills in abundance.

  • Best Time to Visit: Throughout the year

  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport, 100 kilometers/3 hours (approx.)

  • Nearest Railway Station: Ernakulam Railway Station: 104 kilometers/3 hours 19 minutes (approx)
