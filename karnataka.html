KARNATAKA

The state of Karnataka is a land of beautiful places, from its coffee plantations to its wildlife sanctuaries, beaches, magnificent temples. In addition to this, unique cuisines, exciting wildlife, adventurous trekking trails – and this combination makes it a very attractive tourist destination.

October to April is the best time to visit Karnataka. Winters are the best time to go on a wildlife safari or attend the grand Mysore Dussera. Owing to its proximity to the western coast, Karnataka is quite humid during Summers from April to June. While coastal regions are hotter, hill stations are mild and colder.

BANGALORE



  • About:

    • Bangalore is the most cosmopolitan city in India. It is one of the fastest growing industrial and commercial centers in India and is often referred as Silicon Valley of India due to large number of software companies established in the city.

  • Nearest airport:Bengaluru International airport

  • Nearest railway station: Bangalore Railway station

  • places of attraction:Bannerghatta Park, Lalbag Garden, Tipu Sultan Palace, Wonder La Theme park, Aerospace Museum ,VidhanaSoudha

MYSORE



  • About:

    • The city is also known as the cultural capital of Karnataka and is famous for its various temples, palaces, forts and old churches. The town is known for being the birth place for various styles of Indian arts and crafts.

  • Best time to visit:

  • Nearest airport:Bangalore Airport is the nearest airport which is about 184 km from Mysore.

  • Nearest railway station: Mysore Junction Railway station

  • places of attraction:Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill Temple, Mysore Zoo,Srirangapatna,Brindavan Gardens

COORG



  • About:

    • Fondly called the Scotland of India, Coorg is one of the top hill stations in South India.Its natural and lush greenery contributes to its scenic beauty.Coorg is bestowed with aromatic coffee plantations, breathtaking waterfalls, green mountains, and stunning views.

  • Nearest airport:Mangalore airport

  • Nearest railway station:Mysore Junction and Mangalore Junction railway stations

  • places of attraction:Raja's Seat, Abbey Falls, Iruppu Falls, Omkareshwara Temple, Bylakuppe, Talacauvery and Dubare

HAMPI



  • About:

    • Hampi is one of the world’s heritage sites as declared by UNESCO. Situated on the banks of Tungabhadra, the place is home to historical ruins and remains of the Vijayanagar kingdom. Rocks and stone carvings are proof of the skill and mastery of the artisans of the old era.The city has some of the most magnificent buildings and sites that display the architectural brilliance of the Vijaynagar rulers.

  • Nearest airport: Hubli Airport

  • Nearest railway station:Hospet Railway Station

  • places of attraction: Virupaksha Temple, Vittala Temple, and Hampi Bazaar

UDUPI



  • About:

    • Udupi is one of the most famous pilgrimage sites in Karnataka.Udupi, a town near Mangalore sandwiched between the Arabian Sea and Western Ghats is famously known for 2 things- Temples and FoodAccording to local legend, the 27 stars of Hindu astrology were married to the Moon, and soon after, the Moon lost its luster. As Lord Shiva is the last resort for everyone, the Moon and the stars created a Lingam and offered puja. Udu means Lord and Pa means Stars in Sanskrit.

  • Nearest airport:Mangalore Airport

  • Nearest railway station:Udupi Railway Station

  • places of attraction:Sri Krishna Temple,Malpe Beach, Kaup Beach & St. Mary's Island

DANDELI



  • About:

    • Dandeli is a picturesque town on the banks of the River Kali in Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka.It is an adventures hot spot due to its hilly terrain perfect for trekking, its abundant green forests and interesting wildlife.It has a dense forest cover and is one of the best natural habitats in the world

  • Nearest airport:Hubli Airport

  • Nearest railway station: Alnawar railway station

  • places of attraction:Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary,Ulavi Temple, Syntheri Rocks, Kavala Caves, Molangi, Syke's Point, Nagajhari Viewpoint, and Supa Dam

SHIVANASAMUDRA FALLS



  • About:

    • Shivanasamudra water falls, located in the Mandya district of Karnataka is a splendid sight to see especially in the monsoon months. This is a segmented fall, with streams running parallel to each other called Gaganachukki and Bharachukki

  • Best time to visit:October to January

  • Nearest airport:Bangalore Airport

  • Nearest railway station: Mysore Junction
