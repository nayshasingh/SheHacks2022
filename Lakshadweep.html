8 Top Tourist places to visit in Lakshadweep

1. Minicoy Island

The island, also known as Miliku, is one of the most prominent parts of the Lakshadweep Island.The island is the central point of all plans to visit Lakshadeep, or its sister islands. Locked on the southwest side of the archipelago, sprinkled with many attractions. Ideal for viewing the area from Lakshadweep, it has a magnificent lighthouse and many white sand beaches.

Best time to visit: The best season to visit Minicoy Island is winter season and the onset of summers. The favourable months to visit the place are between September to May. The climate remains pleasant during these months offering tourists the best holiday experience

  • Location: Minicoy Island,Lakshadweep,India
  • Timings- open 24 hours

    • How to get there:

  • By air: The nearest airport is the one of Agatti island.
  • By train: The nearest railway station is Kannur railway station.
  • By water: There are the 4 passenger ships; MV Minicoy, MV Tippu Sultan, MV Amindivi and MV Bharat Seema for Minicoy.

    • 2. Marine museum

The museum was created to educate the masses about the importance of aquatic life and to showcase the different species of fish found. There is a shark skeleton here which is one of the most adored. Add this museum to your list of places to visit in Lakshadweep.

    Best time to visit: Can visit it any time of the year.
